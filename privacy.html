Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2024

1. Introduction

Virtual Try On ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Shop Mini application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Images and Photos

When you use Virtual Try On, you may upload or capture photos of yourself to virtually try on products. These images are:

  • Processed temporarily to enable virtual try-on functionality
  • Sent to our backend server for AI/ML processing to overlay products on your image
  • Not stored permanently on our servers after processing is complete
  • Not shared with third parties except as necessary for virtual try-on processing

2.2 Product Images

We may process product images from the Shopify catalog to enable virtual try-on features. These product images are:

  • Used to display products that can be virtually tried on
  • Processed to create virtual try-on overlays
  • Subject to Shopify's data policies

2.3 Error and Usage Data

To improve our service and diagnose issues, we collect:

  • Error logs and crash reports (via Sentry)
  • Technical information such as device type, browser version, and operating system
  • App usage patterns and performance metrics
  • Virtual try-on session data (e.g., which products were tried on, duration of sessions)

This data is anonymized and used solely for improving app functionality.

2.4 What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number) unless you provide it voluntarily
  • Payment information
  • Shipping or billing addresses
  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists or other personal data
  • Biometric data beyond what is necessary for virtual try-on processing

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable virtual try-on functionality by processing your images with product overlays
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of virtual try-on experiences
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Provide product recommendations based on your try-on history
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 AI/ML Processing Services

Your images may be processed using third-party AI/ML services to enable virtual try-on functionality. Images are sent to these services for processing and are subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not store images after processing is complete.

4.2 Sentry

We use Sentry for error tracking and monitoring. Sentry collects technical error data to help us improve the app. See Sentry's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Shopify Shop App

Virtual Try On operates within the Shopify Shop app. Your use of our Mini is also subject to Shopify's privacy practices. Product data and purchase information are handled according to Shopify's policies.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • User photos and images are processed in real-time and not stored permanently on our servers
  • Virtual try-on session data may be temporarily cached for performance optimization
  • Error logs are retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes
  • No personal data is stored beyond what is necessary for app functionality
  • Any cached images are automatically deleted after processing is complete

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure transmission of data using HTTPS
  • API keys and sensitive data stored securely on backend servers
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Encryption of images during transmission
  • Automatic deletion of processed images

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, including any cached images
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out: Choose not to use virtual try-on features that require image processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Virtual Try On is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
